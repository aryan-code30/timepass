FAQ

Quick answers.

Don't see your question? Just call us — a real person picks up.

Do you deliver?

Yes — free curbside pickup at the store, and free hand-delivery within 2 miles for orders over $40. Call the shop and we'll have it at your door within the hour during open hours.

Can I special-order a specific bottle?

Almost always. If a distributor in our state carries it, we can usually get it in within a week. Call or email us with what you're looking for.

Do you do gift wrapping?

We hand-wrap bottles in kraft paper and twine at no extra charge. For something fancier (ribbon, gift box, hand-written card), just ask at the register.

Do you take returns?

State law restricts liquor returns, but if a bottle is corked, flawed, or we steered you wrong, bring it back — we'll make it right.

How do I host a private tasting?

We host private tastings in the back room for groups of 6 – 20. We curate the lineup, you bring the people. Call to check availability and pricing.

Do you sell tobacco or lottery?

We sell lottery tickets at the front register and a small selection of cigars. We don't carry cigarettes.
