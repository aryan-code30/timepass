About Corner Pantry

A little shop with a big cellar.

Corner Pantry is a family-run liquor store tucked into the heart of the neighborhood. We believe great bottles shouldn't be intimidating — so we taste everything we stock, write honest shelf-talkers, and love a good recommendation.

Whether you're picking up a $14 weeknight red or building a wedding bar, we'll treat you like a regular from day one.
